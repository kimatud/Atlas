# Atlas

Atlas is an institutional liquidity analysis engine designed to identify where liquidity exists, determine whether institutions have interacted with that liquidity, and provide objective trade opportunities based on confluence rather than isolated technical patterns.

Atlas is built around one philosophy:

> Markets move to seek liquidity.

Unlike traditional Smart Money Concept indicators, Atlas ranks liquidity, measures institutional intent, scores market structure, and produces confidence-based trade opportunities.
